Boat Models

We have a various models of solar boats, as you wants!

Solar-Powered Electric Boats

These boats run entirely on solar energy, using solar panels to charge electric batteries that power the boat's electric motor. They offer quiet and emission-free cruising.

Solar Catamarans

Catamarans are twin-hulled boats that provide stability and spaciousness. Solar-powered catamarans are designed to harness solar energy for propulsion and onboard systems.

Solar Yachts

Solar yachts are luxurious and high-performance boats that utilize solar power for propulsion and onboard amenities. They are designed for extended cruising and comfort.

Solar Fishing Boats

Fishing boats that utilize solar power to run electric trolling motors and charge fishing equipment, offering a quieter and more sustainable fishing experience.
